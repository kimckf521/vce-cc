## Expected SEO benefits (priority order)

1. **`sitemap.xml` and `robots.txt` exist.** Without these, Google has no efficient crawl plan and may miss/duplicate content. With them, every public page is now declared. Expected impact: faster, cleaner indexing once GSC is connected.
2. **Auth pages can no longer be crawled.** All `(app)/*` routes now cascade `noindex, nofollow`. Eliminates risk of dashboards/admin pages leaking into search results.
3. **Homepage metadata is keyword-rich and AU-localised.** Title and description now include "VCAA past exam questions", "worked solutions", "Year 12 students in Victoria" — high-intent terms students actually search.
4. **Open Graph + Twitter Card render properly.** When the homepage URL is shared on Reddit (r/vce), Discord, Facebook study groups, or X, it now shows a proper preview card instead of a bare URL.
5. **Structured data lets Google understand the entity.** `EducationalOrganization` + `WebSite` JSON-LD tells Google "this is an educational organisation in Australia for students" — affects how the brand appears in knowledge panels and local search.
6. **FAQPage rich result on `/pricing`.** All 5 pricing FAQs are now eligible to appear as expandable rich results in Google for queries like "what's included in vce methods free plan", "can I cancel vce methods subscription", etc.

## Limitations of tonight's run (honest)

1. **`/login`, `/signup`, `/forgot-password`, `/reset-password` still serve `index, follow` in their `<meta>` tags** because they are client components and cannot export `metadata` directly. They ARE blocked from crawling by `robots.txt`, which is the actual line of defence — but the in-page meta tag is technically inconsistent. Phase 2 fix: create `layout.tsx` files for each.
2. **`og-image.png` is referenced but does not exist** in `public/`. The `public/` directory is currently empty. Until an image is added, social shares will show a broken image. **Action item for user:** create a 1200×630 image at `public/og-image.png` (Figma template suggestion below).
3. **`NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL` env var is not set.** Right now the sitemap and JSON-LD use `http://localhost:3000`. **Action item for user:** set this in Vercel project settings to your real production domain. Once set, all SEO URLs become correct automatically.
4. **No GSC / GA4 verification** — needs human action. This is the single most important next step for measurement.
5. **The `/topics`, `/exams`, `/practice` routes are still behind auth.** This is the single biggest organic-search opportunity. A product decision is needed (see 30-day plan, Phase 2).

## Update — scrape defence (added after first-run report)

**Honest assessment (written into the strategy doc):**
